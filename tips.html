Blood Transfusions

It is no secret that the world out there has become very dangerous. And as big of a threat as the Cordyceps Infection is. It isn't the only threat.

Wild animals, the enviroment itself and even other humans represent an ever present threat to us. And although a wound can be bandaged, a fracture can be splinted, and infection prevented. Blood loss can occur quickly if unattended. That's where blood Transfusions come into play. And it can be tricky, because use the wrong blood type and you will be poisoning yourself.

We strongly suggest that you get a Blood Test to determine your blood type, and to carry a spare bag of blood compatible with your own whenever you go out in the wilderness. It could save your life, or even someone elses.

Compatibility is an important thing to consider. Always keep a chart with you in order to prevent a fatal accident:

    O- | Can give to any Blood type
    O+ | Can give to + Blood types
    A- | Can give to A+ / AB- and AB+
    A+ | Can give to AB+
    B- | Can give to B+ / AB- and AB+
    B+ | Can give to AB+
    AB- | Can give to AB+
    AB+ | Can only give Blood to AB+ but can receive any Blood type

A Blood Donation Program is also in place! If you wanna do your part and help others and get paid while at it. Consider making a donation!.

Blood/Price per donation.

    O- | 500.
    O+ | 100.
    A- | 200.
    A+ | 100.
    B- | 200.
    B+ | 100.
    AB- | 100.
    AB+ | 50.

Only One person each can donate blood per day. We will reserve the right to refuse unneeded blood type donations in order to keep our income consistent!

Permits, Licenses & More

FEDRA has implemented a permit/license system in order to keep track of population counts, and general inventory of supplies (weapons, medical supplies) and housing, jobs and good enviromental practices to keep resources sustainable.

If you wish to purchase licenses. Visit the License-Shop. Just find the license/permit you wish to adquire and pay its respective fee. The main licences provided are:

    Hunting & Fishing Permit. 4,000
    Gun License. 6,000
    Medical Marihuana License. 6,000
    Safe Zone Permit. 8,000
    Medical Practitioner License. 10,000
    Business License. 20,000

Remember to keep your licenses up to date and avoid complications on the process.

Property & Businesses

We also provide the opportunity for people to go back in time to the old ways, find a job, provide for their families, get a home of their own, get a vehicle, buy much needed supplies with their hard earned money, and even start a business if they wish to.

Check out the Rent-A-Home if you wish to find a place to call your own. With affordable weekly prices, protection from the elements and illness. Nothing can be better than a roof to lay under after a killer day at work. Please, always adhere to CODE and avoid violations that could result on pentalties or eviction. Any dwelling suspect of violations can be subject to inspection by FEDRA officers.

And when you need, tools, medical supplies or other items for your daily activities. You can always check the Cherno-Trader and Cherno-Chemist and pick from our assortment of items for sale.

However, the trader only have so many items available at all times. Private businesses can offer other products or services that we might not be able to offer due to budget constrains.

If you see anything that is missing and you think you could fill that gap in the supply-chain, we encourage you to start your own business!

Remember to buy your Business License from the License-Shop to avoid penalties and prosecution.

Now sometimes you might find yourself overcumbered with supplies, specially if you are a business owner. Or might need to move long distances with deadlines in consideration. A vehicle is the answer you are looking for!

If you are in need of wheels, check the Cherno-Car-Sales to look at the wide assortment of vehicles, from small to big, you will surely find something that will suit your needs!

Remember to buy a Car Insurance from the License-Shop. Better Safe than Sorry

BANK OF CHERNARUS

Just because the world as we knew it ended doesn't mean you should keep your money under your mattress. That's what the Bank Of Chernarus is for!

Check the Currency-Info channel to learn more about the current currency used on Chernarus. And how to make your deposits and withdraws when you need them.

Check you wallet and account on the Wallet-Channel but beware that some individuals might try and pickpocket you. So always keep your money in the bank whenever possible!

A job board (Work-channel) is also available for you to earn some extra income by doing certain tasks. Some of which can be questionable. Beware with those.

Finally, we have made sure to bring one of the best activities back from the old world. Gambling!. Be sure to pass by Game-Channel if you have some money to burn!
